The Story of Timber and Forge Creations

Who We Are

Timber and Forge Creations was born from a simple belief: beauty often hides in what others overlook. What began as a small workshop with salvaged wood and a few tools has grown into a creative space where nature, metal, resin, and vibrant colors come together to tell new stories.

We see character in the fallen tree that once shaded a neighborhood street, or in the beam reclaimed from a forgotten building. Instead of being discarded, these materials are given new life—shaped into pieces that are bold, functional, and made to last. Steel provides strength, resin brings light and color, and the wood itself carries history. Every creation reflects both nature’s resilience and human imagination.

What We Do

At Timber and Forge, we create sustainable works of art you can live with. Each table, shelf, or custom project is “perfectly imperfect,” blending the raw honesty of natural materials with modern craftsmanship. Our pieces aren’t just furniture or décor—they are reminders that flaws can be beautiful, and that no two stories (or pieces of wood) are ever the same.

How We Work

Everything we make is crafted by hand, not by machines. Every cut, every pour, every polish is done with care and intention. We use high-quality resins and pigments to highlight the grain of the wood, sealing its strength so it can be enjoyed for years to come. No two projects are ever identical—each is designed for the individual who will make it part of their home, their office, or their gathering place.

Why We Do It

We believe that creativity can inspire hope, connection, and joy. By reclaiming and reimagining natural materials, we honor their past while giving them a future. What once seemed broken, flawed, or forgotten becomes a centerpiece of conversation and memory. Our work is about more than building art—it’s about reminding people of the beauty around us, and the possibilities within us.

Our Promise

Working with natural materials means embracing the unexpected. Cracks, knots, or imperfections will always be part of the story, and that’s what makes each piece unique. While styles may echo one another, no finished product will ever be the same. We pour patience, detail, and time into every creation—typically 15 to 30 days—because art worth keeping should never be rushed.

At Timber and Forge, we don’t just make art. We craft stories you can touch, feel, and Always Unforgettable!
